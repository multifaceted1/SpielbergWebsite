Schindler's List

A moving depiction of the true Story of Oskar Schindler, it won multiple academy awards and has been critically acclaimed as one of the best movies of all time.

This black and white film enhances the faces of the actors by playing with light, smoke, shadow, and negative space. The dark subject of the film draws us in as well. There have been many genocides, but the Holocaust haunts us globally with it's methodical, dehumanizing coldness.

Oskar Schindler is an architype of the hero who moves from a superficial existence to one with connection and meaning. As we watch, we reflect on the value of life and the tremendous power a person can wield for good or evil.
