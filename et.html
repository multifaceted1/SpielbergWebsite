E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial

E.T. was the loveable alien that empowered children all over the world to do what was right despite all odds!

One of my very favorite movies, which perfectly speaks to the good and innocent hearts of children. We all hope to be brave, and fight for those we love. We want our loved ones to be healthy. We would like to be connected to the universe. We hope that when we look into the stars there is some magic there.
